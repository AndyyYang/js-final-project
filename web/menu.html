At AHA Food Truck we specialize in Korean cuisines. Korean dishes are known for a balance of plant and animal based products. Many side dishes are fermented and can be distinguished from main dishes. Dairy products are rarely used and meats such as pork, beef, poultry, and fish are often used. Below we have provided our ingredient list and any products that may cause allergies.
